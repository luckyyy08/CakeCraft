INTRODUCTION

This is a small scale project for Online Cake Delivery System (Online Bakery & Cake Ordering System). The basic idea is that user can buy delicious Cakes, Decorations, and Hampers online. It consists of product details, product quantity, and price. The administrator can enter the name and password and generate the report and can perform operations like add category, add and delete the products in the database.

The Online Bakery & Cake Ordering System Online Shopping system enables store managers to set up online shops, customers to browse through the shops, maintain lists of product categories. Also the agenda is to design the online shopping site to manage the items in the shop and also help customers to purchase the product online without visiting the shop physically which saves the time of the customer.

The customer can give the acknowledgment of the product by the review system that whether the product was useful or not which will also be helpful for the admin to improve services.

PROBLEM DEFINITION

User will visit cake shop or bakery to buy products. It requires lot of time and effort to travel and choose. The proposed system will handle this problem. Online purchasing is done.

Problem Statement: This project aims to develop an online shopping platform for customers. With the help of this you can carry out an online shopping from your home. There is no compelling reason to go to the crowded stores or shopping centers during festival seasons or busy hours.

You simply require a mobile and one payment sending option to shop online. To get to this online shopping system all the user will need to have an email and password to login and proceed your shopping. The login credentials for an online shopping system are under high security and nobody will have the capacity to crack it easily.

Upon successful login the user can purchase a wide range of bakery products, customized cakes, and party decorations as per the users requirement. And of course you will get your requested ordered items at your door step. No need to go physical shops with this you will have more time to spend with your family.

Shopkeeper has to build a shop which requires lots of money but through Online Bakery & Cake Ordering System he can easily sale his products without wasting much money on physical infrastructure. Manually it is very difficult to manage the inventory and orders. With the help of computer it becomes easy and faster to manage the system.

NEED OF SYSTEM

The implementation of an online bakery and cake shopping system addresses various needs and demands, both for consumers and businesses. Here are some key reasons highlighting the need for such a system:

1. Convenience:
• 24/7 Accessibility: Online shopping allows users to browse and make purchases at any time, providing the convenience of round-the-clock access to products.
• Human errors are reduced.

2. Product Variety and Availability:
• Online platforms can showcase a vast range of baked goods, cakes, and gifts, providing customers with a wide selection.
• Customers can easily check the availability of products and receive updates on stock status.

3. Time Savings:
• Users can quickly find products using search and filter options, saving time compared to traditional retail visiting.

4. Cost Savings:
• Customers save on transportation costs associated with visiting physical stores.

5. Order Tracking and Transparency:
• Customers can track the status of their orders in real-time, providing transparency and reducing anxiety about delivery.

6. Efficient Returns and Exchanges:
• Online platforms typically have clear and user-friendly return policies. Customer support services are also readily available to assist users with any issues or inquiries they may have.

7. Promotions and Discounts:
• Online platforms frequently offer promotions, discounts, and exclusive deals, attracting price-conscious consumers and providing them with cost-effective shopping options.

8. Convenient Payment Options:
• Online platforms offer a variety of payment options, including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and other secure payment gateways. This flexibility enhances the ease of completing transactions.
